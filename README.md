# 📊 Sales Dashboard & Business Intelligence Analysis

**Author:** Miral Butt | Data Analyst | MPhil Statistics, LCWU Lahore

---

## 🎯 Project Overview

This project performs complete **Sales Analytics** on retail data covering 4 regions and 4 product categories over 12 months — answering key business questions through data visualization and statistical analysis.

---

## 🔍 Business Questions Answered

- Which region generates the most revenue?
- Which products are best sellers?
- What are the monthly sales trends?
- Where should marketing budget be focused?

---

## 📈 Key KPIs

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Best Region | North |
| Best Product | Electronics |
| Peak Season | November & December |
| Growth Pattern | Q4 outperforms Q1 consistently |
